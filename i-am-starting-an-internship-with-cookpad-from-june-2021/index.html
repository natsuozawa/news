I am starting an internship with Cookpad from June 2021

May 21, 2021

I will be working at Cookpad as a software engineer intern for the summer of 2021!

About the Internship

Cookpad is a Japanese food tech company. At Cookpad, I plan to work on the search system of the main recipe sharing service, to improve the user experience for exploring new recipes.

Goals

My main goals in this internship are to

  1. Gain skills and experience in developing efficient and scalable systems handling large amounts of data and traffic
  2. Explore the values of working as a software engineer at Cookpad
  3. Contribute to a more diverse and exciting user search experience on Cookpad
