I will be providing teaching support for Introduction to Computation

October 1, 2021

I will be a teaching support provider for the Introduction to Computation course as a tutor, lab demonstrator, and marker. The course will run through the first semester of 2021-2022 at the University of Edinburgh, taught by Prof. Philip Wadler and Dr. Julian Bradfield. The course focuses on functional programming and computational logic.
