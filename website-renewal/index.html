Website Renewal

April 15, 2023

A renewal for the Natsu Ozawa website has been long overdue, and I have finally completed it as a weekend project.

This website was designed in 2018, using primitive technologies like jQuery and Sass. This was the time they started releasing new JavaScript web frameworks every year, and I decided to utilize technologies that stand the test of time. Owing to this, most of it has been easy to maintain - I am not required to fix the tens of thousands of security vulnerabilities that are reported on my dependencies. However, some of the CSS I have written in my high school years were quite bad. I wish I had ChatGPT back then.

It is also worth mentioning that all of the images originally used in this website are now decomissioned. They are now replaced with a DALL E image. I'm excited to see what the next five years will take us with technology.
